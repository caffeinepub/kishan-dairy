# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create an e-commerce website for Kishan Dairy that allows customers in Sitamarhi, Bihar to browse dairy products and place orders.

**Planned changes:**
- Display "Kishan Dairy" branding in header with logo
- Show prominent delivery notice: "Delivery available only in Sitamarhi, Bihar"
- Create product catalog displaying dairy products (milk, curd, paneer, butter, ghee) with images, names, prices, and units
- Implement shopping cart where users can add products, adjust quantities, and view total
- Build checkout form collecting customer name, phone number, and delivery address
- Store orders with customer details and cart items in backend
- Design with warm, earthy color palette (greens, creams, browns) evoking fresh farm products
- Serve generated images from frontend/public/assets/generated

**User-visible outcome:** Customers can browse Kishan Dairy's product catalog, add items to cart, and place orders for delivery in Sitamarhi, Bihar with a clean, trustworthy interface.
